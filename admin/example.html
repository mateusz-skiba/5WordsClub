atmosphere

The feeling or mood in a place, especially as you experience it.

Example atmospheres

Forms The atmosphere at the concert was amazing.

Collocations create an atmosphere, atmosphere of excitement

concentrate

To focus your mind or attention on something.

Example concentrates, concentrated, concentrating

Forms She needs to concentrate on her homework.

Collocations concentrate on, concentrate hard

particular

Used to talk about one specific thing or person, not general.

Example particularly

Forms I don’t have a particular plan for the weekend.

Collocations in particular, particular attention

luxury

Something expensive or special that is not necessary but makes life more enjoyable.

Example luxuries

Forms They live in a house with many luxuries.

Collocations luxury goods, live in luxury

interview

A meeting where someone asks questions to find out information, or to get a job.

Example interviews, interviewed, interviewing

Forms She has an interview for a new job tomorrow.

Collocations job interview, interview for a position
